Bio

PAT NASTY is a true open format DJ, known for his ability to seamlessly switch between sounds, genres, crowds and venues. Using his expert song selection and mixing skills; his driving force while performing is to create a party vibe that captivates and compels his audience to dance and have a great time.

PAT NASTY is a founding member of 'The Good Guys' crew, and he is also a DJ for Seattle radio station 'MOViN 92.5'. He's been DJing since 2008, and his style is heavily influenced by his roots in hip hop and turntablism.
